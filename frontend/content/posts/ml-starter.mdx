### Books

1. **"Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow" by Aurélien Géron**
- Description: This book provides a practical guide to machine learning using Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow. It covers a wide range of topics, from data preprocessing to advanced deep learning techniques.
- [Link to Book](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/hands-on-machine-learning/9781492032632/)

2. **"Machine Learning Yearning" by Andriy Burkov**
- Description: A comprehensive book that covers essential machine learning concepts and algorithms. It's suitable for beginners and experienced practitioners.
- [Link to Book](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/machine-learning-yearning/9781492077356/)

3. **Deep Learning by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio and Aaron Courville**
- Description: This Deep Learning textbook is a resource intended to help students and practitioners enter the field of machine learning in general, and deep learning in particular.
- [Link to Book](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/)

### Online Courses

1. **"Introduction to TensorFlow for Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning" by deeplearning.ai**
- Description: This Coursera course offers an introduction to TensorFlow, covering its fundamentals, usage in AI and machine learning, and building deep learning models.
- [Link to Course](https://www.coursera.org/learn/introduction-tensorflow)

2. **"Machine Learning" by Stanford University (Coursera)**
- Description: Taught by Andrew Ng, this highly popular course covers a wide range of machine learning topics, including neural networks, deep learning, and various algorithms.
- [Link to Course](https://www.coursera.org/learn/machine-learning)

3. **Deep Learning Specialization by DeepLearning.AI**
- In five courses, you will learn the foundations of Deep Learning, understand how to build neural networks, and learn how to lead successful machine learning projects and build a career in AI. You will master not only the theory, but also see how it is applied in industry.
- [Link to Course](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/deep-learning)

### Math Resources

1. **"Mathematics for Machine Learning Spelization" by Imperial College London**
- Description: A comprehensive series of online textbooks that covers the mathematical foundations of machine learning, including linear algebra, calculus, and probability.
- [Link to Textbook](https://mml-book.github.io/)

2. **Essence of Calculus by 3Blue1Brown**
- Description: A series of short, visual videos from 3blue1brown that explain the fundamentals of calculus in a way that give you a strong understanding of the fundamental theorems, and not just how the equations work.
- [Link to Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDMsr9K-rj53DwVRMYO3t5Yr)
